# Privacy Policy for The Paragliding App

**Last Updated: 2025-01-12**

## Overview
The Paragliding App is committed to protecting your privacy. All your flight data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

## Data Collection and Storage

**What we collect:**
- Flight data you import (IGC files, sites, wings, notes)
- Device location (only when you use the "Nearby Sites" feature)
- App preferences and settings

**Where it's stored:**
- All data is stored locally on your device
- No data is transmitted to or stored on external servers
- Backups remain under your control

## Third-Party Services

The app connects to external services to provide features:
- **Paragliding Earth API**: To fetch flying site information
- **OpenAIP API**: For aviation airspace and airport data
- **Weather forecast providers**: For site-specific weather forecasts
- **OpenStreetMap**: For map tiles and terrain data

These services may log your IP address and request metadata according to their own privacy policies.

## Permissions

- **Storage**: Read IGC files and save backups
- **Location**: Find nearby flying sites (optional)
- **Internet**: Fetch maps, sites, airspace, and weather data

## Your Rights

- You own all your data
- You can export or delete your data at any time
- No account registration required
- No analytics or tracking
